# Hymn Index Verification Checklist
## Hymnals of the Adventist Movement — SDA Hymnal (1985)

The app's 695-hymn index was compiled from hymnary.org (the academic hymn
database) and cross-checked against sdahymns.net and the published section
structure. Before launch, we ask beta testers to spot-check it against a
physical 1985 SDA Hymnal. **Grab the hymnal from your pew or shelf!**

## How to verify (5 minutes per tester)
1. Open the app and pick the range assigned to you below.
2. For each hymn number, check the TITLE in the app matches the hymnal.
3. Report ANY mismatch (even small wording) to: coolrunnings7@gmail.com
   Format: "Hymn 123 — app says X, hymnal says Y"
